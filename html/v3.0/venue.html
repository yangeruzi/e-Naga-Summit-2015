Nagaland Baptist Churches Council (NBCC) Convention Center

NBCC constructed a new convention centre to celebrate its Platinum Jubilee in 2012. About 10,000 delegates, including foreign delegates from the USA, Japan, South Korea, Singapore and cultural troupes from Thailand participated in the convention. The convention centre is designed to accomodate 7000 people.

How to get here

Mode of Travel, Accomodation, Weather:

Nearest Airport is Dimapur, located 74 Km from Kohima. After reaching Dimapur, travel to Kohima must be made by Road and the journey to kohima takes approximately 2 hours
