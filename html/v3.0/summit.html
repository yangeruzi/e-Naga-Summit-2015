OBJECTIVES

‘Ideas and Solutions to Transform North-East India’ has been selected as the theme for the e-Naga Summit 2015. The theme has been broadly selected for participants and resource persons to analyse and identify the root cause of the challenges and issues faced in the North-East which will help the forum to ideate and bring out practical solutions to bring about a radical transformation in the region.

With the advent of Digital India under which e-Governance initiatives will play a key role to transform the country with increased transparency, efficient and cost effective public service delivery and providing better access, equity & social empowerment to our citizens.

Our country is witnessing a paradigm shift in Governance, Commerce, Media and all other spheres of life where Information Technology and electronic media is playing a transformative role towards building a knowledge–led economy. The world is looking up to India to drive the global economic growth and India is looking east towards the North Eastern States with the Look East Policy promising an opportunity to open our eastern corridors towards South East Asia and boost economic growth and progress. It is time for the North Eastern States to unleash our potential by networking, collaborating and initiating innovative solutions by focussing on ICT as an enabler for radical transformation.

The Department of Information Technology and Communication, Government of Nagaland has taken the initiative to organize the ‘e-Naga Summit’ starting from this year with the objective to bring together Entrepreneurs, Academia, Industry and Government under one roof. This summit is envisioned to build the foundation required to identify our existing gaps, incubate ideas and devise solutions specific to the needs of the North-East region which will ultimately lead to progress and prosperity of the nation as a whole.

KEY THEMES

e-Naga Summit will engage the best minds from the four pillars of the summit Academia, Industry, Government and Entrepreneurs to empower the people with new ideas, innovations and solutions to transform the region through ICT with key focus on the following:-

Infrastructure, Power and Internet Connectivity
Software Technology Parks
Technical Education
Business Process Outsourcing Industry
IT innovators and Entrepreneurs
Women Empowerment
